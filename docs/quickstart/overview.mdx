## User Dashboard

**Step 1:** Once you have logged in, you will need to create a new App and select the protocol you want to use.
All apps must have at least one protocol.

<img
  src={img1}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## Create an App

**Step 2:** Give your App a name, select the environment - **Production** or **Staging** to differentiate between your apps' state, then select the protocol.

<img
  src={img2}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## API Keys

**Step 3:** After creating the App, you will get access to the API key under the **Overview** tab where you can view, copy, create and delete your API keys.

<img
  src={img3}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## Protocols

**Step 4:** Clicking on the **Protocols** tab, you will be able to see all the protocols that you are currently subscribed to. Here you can add and remove additional protocols as needed (up to the maximum that your App plan allows).

<img
  src={img4}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## Endpoints

**Step 5:** When viewing a protocol, you can click on the **Example** link to see sample requests for that endpoint.

<img
  src={img5}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## Analytics

**Step 6:** You can click on the **Analytics** tab to get access to your Request Logs. [Read more about Analytics and Request Logs](/quickstart/app-analytics-and-request-logs).

<img
  src={img8}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## Team Management

**Step 7:** To invite your team members and collaborate with them on the project, click on the **Settings** tab, then click **Team** on the left side of the page. [Read more about App team roles and permissions](/quickstart/settings-and-security#adding-collaborators)

<img
  src={img7}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## App Settings

**Step 8:** To manage the payment method for your App, check invoices and delete apps, etc. click on the **Settings** tab. This is also where you can add CORS Allowed Origins, and manage your Team by adding or removing Collaborators.

<img
  src={img6}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>
