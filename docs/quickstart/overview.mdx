## User Dashboard

**Step 1:** Click on **Create New App** and select a protocol&edash;all apps must have at least one protocol.

<img
  src={img1}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## Create an App

**Step 2:** Give your app a name. Select the intended environment&mdash;**Production** or **Staging**&mdash;, then select the protocol.

<img
  src={img2}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## API Keys

**Step 3:** After creating the app, you will have access to the API key under the **Overview** tab where you can view, copy, create, and delete your API keys.

<img
  src={img3}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## Protocols

**Step 4:** Under the **Protocols** tab, you will be able to see all the protocols to which you are currently subscribed. Add and remove additional protocols as needed (up to the maximum that your app plan allows).

<img
  src={img4}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## Endpoints

**Step 5:** When viewing a protocol, you can click on the **Example** link to see sample requests for that endpoint.

<img
  src={img5}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## Analytics

**Step 6:** Select the **Analytics** tab to access your request logs. [Read more about Analytics and Request Logs](/quickstart/app-analytics-and-request-logs).

<img
  src={img8}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## Team Management

**Step 7:** Invite your team members by selecting the **Settings** tab, then clicking **Team** on the left side of the page. [Read more about app team roles and permissions](/quickstart/settings-and-security#adding-collaborators)

<img
  src={img7}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## App Settings

**Step 8:** Also under the **Settings** tab, you may manage your payment method and your app plan. You can also add CORS allowed origins and delete your app if you need to. 

<img
  src={img6}
  alt="DataHub Dashboard"
  width="400"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>
