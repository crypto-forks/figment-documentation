## From the App Overview

From the Dashboard, click on your app. Click **Add Protocol** at the top right of the page.

<img
  src={img1}
  alt="Add a Protocol"
  width="700"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

You can pick your new protocol from the list. Click **Add Protocol** at the bottom of the page to continue.

<img
  src={img2}
  alt="Choose a Protocol"
  width="700"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## From the Protocols Tab

From the Dashboard, select your app. Under the **Protocols** tab, click on **Add Protocol** at the top right of the page.

<img
  src={img3}
  alt="Choose a Protocol"
  width="700"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>

## Removing Protocols

From the Dashboard, select your app. Under the **Protocols** tab, click on the three dots on the right side of a protocol to display the **Delete** action.

Clicking on **Delete** will immediately remove the protocol (including associated analytics / logging data) from your app.

  src={img4}
  alt="Delete a Protocol"
  width="700"
  className="shadow--tl inline-image"
/>
